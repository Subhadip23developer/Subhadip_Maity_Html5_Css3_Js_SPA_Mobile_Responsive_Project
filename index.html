About Us

Sweet Home Furniture is India’s premium furniture brand in both home and institutional segments with a strong commitment to sustainability and centers of excellence in design, manufacturing and retail. Our journey was started in 2015 with 50 people and now 2022 we are one of the most trustable and reputed company in the world. Currently present in over 30 cities with 25 companies owned stores and over 100 dealers. Sweet Home Furniture aims to transform spaces with its thoughtfully designed furniture to create brighter homes and offices with products that have the highest design quotient in aesthetics, functionality and technology.
